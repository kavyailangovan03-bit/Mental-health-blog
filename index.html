The Balance Between Work and Well-being

A visual exploration of employee stress, productivity, and remote work balance.

About the Data

The dataset features insights from 20 employees across departments like Marketing, Sales, HR, IT, and Design. It tracks their weekly work hours, happiness and stress levels, remote work ratios, and productivity performance. This data was designed to uncover how different work conditions — from long hours to flexible setups — influence both mental well-being and efficiency. Through the visuals below, we highlight department stress averages and the link between remote work and productivity trends.
