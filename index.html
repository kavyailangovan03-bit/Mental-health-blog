The Balance Between Work and Well-being

A focused dashboard exploring stress and productivity patterns.

About the Data

This dataset contains 20 anonymized employee records across departments (Marketing, Sales, HR, IT, Design). Fields include weekly work hours, happiness and stress scores (1–10), percentage remote work, tenure, gender, age, and productivity scores. The visuals below highlight department-level stress and the relationship between remote work and productivity to inform wellbeing and hybrid work policies.
