The Balance Between Work and Well-being

A modern visualization exploring employee stress, productivity, and remote work balance.

About the Data

This dataset captures 20 anonymized employee records across five departments: Marketing, Sales, HR, IT, and Design. Each record contains weekly work hours, happiness and stress scores, remote work percentages, experience in years, gender, age, and productivity ratings. The data provides a snapshot of workplace well-being — highlighting how workload, job roles, and flexibility contribute to both mental health and performance outcomes. Through the following charts, we uncover stress patterns across departments and the correlation between remote work and productivity.
