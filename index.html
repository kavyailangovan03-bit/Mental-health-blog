About the dataset

This dataset contains anonymized records for 20 employees across several departments (Marketing, Sales, HR, IT, Design). Each record includes weekly work hours, self-reported happiness and stress scores (1–10), percentage of time spent working remotely, tenure in years, gender, age, and a productivity score. The data is synthetic but realistic — designed to explore relationships between remote work, stress and productivity and to inform employee wellbeing strategies.
